We are solving a crucial data privacy and security related problem.

We are creating a unique blockchain payment service runned by $eTELLER, our utility token.

Our mission is to create a blockchain powered payment system targeted at addressing financial transaction challenges & limitations of financial sector & payment service providers expecially in Africa. We will provide blockchain enhance payment services facilitating fast, secure, flexible, low-cost domestic & international payments while connecting global financial systems. The high level of security presented by a distributed ledger system offers enormous benefits to establishing a secure data network. Business offering services in consumer products and services adopt blockchain technology to secure consumers data record. Data tempering & breach has become a menace in Africa, with most parties entrusted with the task of protecting sensitive documents e.g. Wills, election results, examination results, certificates are not living up to their expectation. Taking advantage of blockchain immutability, Escrow Teller aims to Solve data tempering in Africa, offering rapid advancements in data security by implementing strong authentication and cryptography key vaulting mechanisms.
